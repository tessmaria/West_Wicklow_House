About Us

The West Wicklow House is a traditional Irish pub located in the heart of Co. Wicklow, Ireland. Originally built as a couch house to shelter travellers overnight, The West Wicklow House is now a busy, vibrant restaurant pub serving quality food. Boasting a fantastic interior, large outdoor area and fantastic staff, The West Wicklow House is a great choice to welcome families, cyclists and anyone else.

With so many nearby treasures including Blessington Lakes and Greenway, Russborough House, Glendalough and the Wicklow Mountains (to name only a few!) The West Wicklow House is the perfect spot to stop for a delicious meal and a drink.
